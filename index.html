KIET ROBOTICS LAB

Built from Scratch.

KIET Robotics Lab (KRL) was established in 2022 with a single mission — to build real robots with real impact. Starting from scratch with no proper equipment, our team hand-built a rover capable of carrying 50 kg. We are now working toward bringing a humanoid robot and a robot dog into the lab for advanced research projects. Lead by Y. Sai and Krishna, KRL is where engineering meets ambition.
