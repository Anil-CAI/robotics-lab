Portfolio

Key Contributions

Heavy Load Lifting Rover

A custom-built heavy-duty rover designed to carry payloads of up to 50kg. Engineered from the ground up, this project marked the beginning of KRL's hardware capabilities, focusing on robust chassis design and power distribution.

Multi-Floor Autonomous Navigation

Developing waypoint navigation for the Go2W robot across multiple floors. Implementation uses tightly coupled lidar-inertial odometry for precise localization without GPS.

Unitree G1 RL Training

R&D on Reinforcement Learning training for the Unitree G1 humanoid robot, focusing on stable locomotion and complex gait patterns in dynamic environments.

Native Telugu AI

A groundbreaking speech recognition and answering system in native Telugu language specifically integrated into the G1 robot, allowing for localized human-robot interaction.

Go2W Autonomous Systems

Implementation of tightly coupled lidar-inertial odometry for the Unitree Go2W, enabling flawless navigation in unstructured indoor environments.
